Horse's Neck

Category: Ordinary drink
Alcohol: Alcoholic
Glass: Highball glass
Ingredients:

  • Lemon peel:

    1 long strip

  • Brandy:

    2 oz

  • Ginger ale:

    5 oz

  • Bitters:

    2 dashes
Recipe:

Pour brandy and ginger ale directly into highball glass with ice cubes. Stir gently. Garnish with lemon zest. If desired, add dashes of Angostura Bitter.
